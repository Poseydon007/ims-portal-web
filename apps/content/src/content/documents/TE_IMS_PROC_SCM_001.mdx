<h2 id="s1" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">1. Purpose</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This procedure establishes standardised, safe, and environmentally responsible controls for delivery, handling, and supply of critical site consumables at True East Mining Company (TEMC) remote drilling and exploration operations — diesel fuel, lubricating oils, drilling fluid chemicals, spare parts, and general site consumables. It governs supplier qualification, mobile delivery, on-site transfer, waste return, and record-keeping. It is aligned with ISO 9001:2015 (Clause 8.4 Externally Provided Processes, Products and Services), ISO 14001:2015, ISO 45001:2018, and KSA regulations (NCEC, MEWA, MHRSD).</p>

<h2 id="s2" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">2. Scope</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This procedure applies to all on-site supply operations at TEMC drilling sites — mobile diesel refueling, oil delivery and used-oil collection, drilling fluid chemical delivery, consumables and spare parts. All deliveries occur via approved mobile units; no on-site bulk storage unless specifically engineered, permitted, and bunded. It applies to rig crews, shift supervisors, logistics, maintenance, mud engineers, HSE, and all third-party suppliers operating on TEMC premises.</p>

<h2 id="s3" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">3. Definitions</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Mobile Delivery — fuel, oil, or chemical delivery by licensed road tanker or bowser directly to the point of use.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Approved Supplier — a supplier formally qualified and on the TEMC Approved Supplier List, with valid commercial registration, HSE records, and certifications.</span></li>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Waste Manifest — the official NCEC / MEWA document tracking transfer of hazardous waste (used oil, chemicals) to a licensed contractor.</span></li>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">

</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>SDS — Safety Data Sheet — hazard, handling, and emergency information for every chemical on site.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s5" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">5. Key Principles</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Planned refueling, oil change, and major fluid / chemical transfers occur during the shift handover window.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>If fuel or fluid levels fall critically mid-shift, operations are fully suspended and the pre-refueling procedure (safety meeting, 10 m zone, grounding) is followed exactly as for a scheduled event.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>All operations with ignition or spill risk are stopped during transfers.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Only environmentally friendly, low-toxicity, biodegradable drilling chemicals are permitted.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Used oil is recycled exclusively via NCEC / MEWA licensed third-party contractors.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Full HSE controls apply: PPE, spill kits, grounding, bunded containment, emergency response.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s6" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">6. Supplier Qualification (ISO 9001 Clause 8.4)</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Fuel and chemical suppliers additionally provide SDS (Arabic and English) and NCEC / MEWA licences where applicable.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Approved Supplier List reviewed annually or after any major non-conformance.</span></li>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">

<h2 id="s7" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">7. Diesel Refueling via Mobile Unit</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">Refueling is performed per SOP-MAINT-002 Refueling and Fuel Handling. Key controls:</p>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Outgoing supervisor notifies Logistics ≥1 hour ahead.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Rig operations stop 10 minutes prior.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Non-essential personnel cleared (10 m radius); signs and barriers posted.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Mandatory 5-minute safety meeting — hazards, procedure, emergency response.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Logistics inspects the mobile truck (leaks, fittings, spill kit, fire extinguishers, certification).</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Truck positioned ≥5 m from rig; wheel chocks applied; brake engaged.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Bonding and grounding applied between truck and rig / earth.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Drip trays, absorbent pads, and secondary containment placed under all connections.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Slow transfer using drip-free nozzles and overfill protection; continuous monitoring.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>No smoking, open flames, or mobile phones within 20 m.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Post-refueling inspection; Logistics records volume, time, personnel, issues.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Operations resume only after final clearance by incoming supervisor and Logistics.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s8" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">8. Oil Change and Fluid Maintenance</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">Engine, water pump (crankcase / gearbox), and hydraulic oil changes follow OEM intervals, runtime hours, or condition-based triggers.</p>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Pre-change — schedule per hours meter / planned maintenance; notify Logistics for oil delivery or waste collection; LOTO the equipment ≥15–30 minutes prior (cooling); set up contained work area with impermeable mats, bunds, spill kits; toolbox talk.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Change — chemical-resistant PPE; collection containers under drain points; drain completely; replace filters; clean breathers, strainers, fill points; refill slowly with OEM-approved fluid; monitor for leaks.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Post-change — brief test run to circulate fluid and check for leaks; label and seal waste drums; record volumes, oil types, condition notes, runtime.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s9" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">9. Used Oil Disposal and Recycling</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>All used lubricating oils are classified as hazardous waste.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>On-site storage — transfer to labelled, closed drums immediately; store in a bunded area (110% capacity) away from drains and ignition sources; daily visual inspection; maximum 90 days storage.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Collection — Maintenance Lead notifies Logistics when drums reach ~80% or monthly. Logistics schedules an NCEC / MEWA licensed contractor. Supervised transfer using drip trays.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Manifest — both parties sign the official waste manifest — waste type, volume, date, signatures.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Recycling — contractor transports to a licensed facility for dewatering, filtering, and re-refining. Certificate of recycling provided.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Records — Logistics retains manifests and certificates for ≥5 years.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s10" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">10. Drilling Chemicals</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Selection — HSE-approved list only — xanthan gum, PAC/CMC, bentonite, barite (where allowed), vegetable-based lubricants, glycerin shale inhibitors, biopolymers.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Documentation — full SDS/MSDS (Arabic and English) on site for every chemical.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Delivery — same mobile delivery controls as diesel — inspection, ground/bond, drip trays, containment, slow supervised transfer, 20 m no-ignition zone.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>On-site handling — designated bunded mixing area with liner, eyewash, spill kits; PPE; gradual addition to mud pit / hopper; continuous mud property monitoring; toolbox talks before major additions.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Empty containers — sacks, totes, and IBCs returned to Logistics for proper disposal / recycling — no on-site burning or burial.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s12" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">12. HSE Controls (All Supply Activities)</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Job Safety Analysis (JSA) before each critical task.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Mandatory PPE: flame-resistant clothing, nitrile gloves, safety goggles, steel-toe boots, respiratory protection when required.</span></li>

<h2 id="s1" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">1. Purpose</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">To establish a consistent framework for identifying, analysing, evaluating, treating and monitoring risks and opportunities affecting True East Mining's operations, personnel, assets, environment and business objectives. This procedure operationalises ISO 9001:2015 Clause 6.1, ISO 14001:2015 Clause 6.1 and ISO 45001:2018 Clause 6.1 — risk-based thinking across the full IMS.</p>

<h2 id="s2" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">2. Scope</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This procedure applies across all True East Mining activities, sites, projects, functions and processes, including strategic, operational, HSE, environmental, financial, compliance, contractor and reputational risks. It covers both hazardous risks (negative effects) and opportunities (positive effects) of uncertainty.</p>

<h2 id="s3" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">3. Definitions</h2>

  <table class="w-full text-sm border-collapse">

<h2 id="s6" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">6. Risk Management Process</h2>

<h3 class="text-sm font-bold mt-5 mb-2" style="color: #081C2E;">6.1 Establish the Context</h3>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">Define internal and external factors influencing objectives — SWOT, PESTEL, interested parties (ISO 9001 Clauses 4.1 and 4.2), stakeholder requirements, KSA regulatory environment, contractual obligations, operational constraints and strategic direction.</p>

<h3 class="text-sm font-bold mt-5 mb-2" style="color: #081C2E;">6.2 Identify Risks and Opportunities</h3>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">Risks and opportunities must be identified systematically using a combination of techniques:</p>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Job Hazard Analysis (JHA) and Take 5 cards at task level.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Formal HIRA (Hazard Identification and Risk Assessment) workshops for drilling, core handling, driving, hot work and all high-risk tasks.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Management review inputs, audit findings and incident investigations.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Supplier, contractor and interested-party feedback.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Environmental aspects and impacts register (ISO 14001 Clause 6.1.2).</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Legal and regulatory horizon scanning (MHRSD, NCEC, Ministry of Energy, Ministry of Industry and Mineral Resources).</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Market, commercial, financial and reputational scanning by MD and Finance.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>&quot;Over the horizon&quot; scanning — emerging technologies, climate trends, political and regulatory change.</span></li>
</ul>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">For each risk, describe the Event, Cause and Impact. Assess existing controls, their effectiveness, then rate Likelihood and Severity.</p>

<h3 class="text-sm font-bold mt-5 mb-2" style="color: #081C2E;">6.4 Evaluate Risks</h3>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">The risk level determines whether further treatment is required. Outputs of this phase are: (a) a prioritised risk register, (b) identified treatment actions, and (c) residual risk assessment. Where an action differs from the standard requirement for the risk category, deviation must be authorised in writing by the Managing Director.</p>

<h3 class="text-sm font-bold mt-5 mb-2" style="color: #081C2E;">6.5 Treat Risks</h3>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">Treatment options (apply the hierarchy of controls for HSE risks):</p>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Avoid — change business process, scope or objective to eliminate the risk (highest priority for HIGH/EXTREME HSE risks).</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Reduce Likelihood — implement preventive controls that reduce the probability of the event.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Reduce Consequence — implement mitigative controls that reduce the impact if the event occurs.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Share / Transfer — transfer ownership and liability through insurance, contracts or partnerships where appropriate.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Retain — accept the risk when it is within appetite and cost of further treatment exceeds benefit.</span></li>
</ul>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">Each treatment action requires: responsible person, realistic due date, cost allocation, and performance measure. Actions are tracked in the Risk and Opportunity Register (TE-IMS-REG-HSE-008).</p>

<h3 class="text-sm font-bold mt-5 mb-2" style="color: #081C2E;">6.6 Monitor and Review</h3>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Risk Owners monitor their assigned risks continuously and report status monthly to the HSE Manager.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>HSE Manager reviews the full Risk and Opportunity Register quarterly and escalates HIGH/EXTREME risks to the Site Manager and Managing Director.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Management Review (TE-IMS-PROC-SYS-002) receives a summary of top risks, treatment progress and emerging risks.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Internal Audit (TE-IMS-PROC-SYS-004) tests the effectiveness of controls on HIGH risks.</span></li>
</ul>

<h3 class="text-sm font-bold mt-5 mb-2" style="color: #081C2E;">6.7 Opportunities</h3>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">The same process applies to opportunities. Where an opportunity is identified (improved technology, new contract, process efficiency, commercial advantage), it is logged in the Risk and Opportunity Register, evaluated for feasibility and benefit, and converted to an improvement action via TE-IMS-PROC-SYS-005 Continual Improvement and CAPA.</p>

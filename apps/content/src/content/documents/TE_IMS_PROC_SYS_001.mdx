<h2 id="s1" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">1. PURPOSE</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">{"This procedure establishes the mandatory, standardized process for the creation, review, approval, distribution, revision, storage, retrieval, and disposal of all controlled documents and records within True East Mining Company's Integrated Management System (IMS). It ensures:"}</p>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>All documents are unique, current, legible, readily identifiable, and available at points of use.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Only current versions are used across the organisation.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Obsolete documents are removed from use or clearly identified as obsolete.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Document changes are controlled and traceable through revision history.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Records are protected, retained, and disposed of appropriately.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Full compliance with ISO 9001:2015 (clause 7.5), ISO 14001:2015 (clause 7.5), and ISO 45001:2018 (clause 7.5).</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s2" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">2. SCOPE</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This procedure applies to all controlled documents and records within the True East Mining IMS, including:</p>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Policies, procedures, SOPs and work instructions.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Forms, registers, checklists and templates.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Records (completed forms, reports, minutes, logs).</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Drawings, certificates and legal registers.</span></li>
</ul>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">Exclusions:</p>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Finance &amp; accounting documents — handled separately per TE-IMS-REF-SYS-002 (Treatment of Finance &amp; Accounting Documents).</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Personal employee files (HR confidential records) — managed under the HR confidentiality policy.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s3" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">3. DEFINITIONS</h2>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Controlled Document — Any document that requires formal approval, unique identification, and version control (e.g., procedures, SOPs, forms).</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Record — Evidence of completed activities (e.g., signed checklists, reports, minutes, training registers).</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Master Document Register — The central list of all controlled documents with codes, revisions, status and locations, maintained under TE-IMS-REG-SYS-001.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Obsolete Document — Any document superseded by a newer revision or formally withdrawn from use.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s4" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">4. RESPONSIBILITIES</h2>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>IMS Project Lead / Document Controller — Maintain the Master Document Register, enforce numbering rules, issue new codes, track revisions, and distribute updates.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Department Heads / Process Owners — Author, review and approve documents in their area; ensure current versions are used; remove obsolete copies from circulation.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>All Users — Use only current, approved documents; report obsolete or missing documents; never alter controlled documents without approval.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>CEO / Managing Director — Approve top-level policies and major changes to the IMS framework.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s6" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">6. CREATION &amp; APPROVAL</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">All new controlled documents shall follow the four-step creation and approval workflow:</p>

<h2 class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">1. Draft — Author creates the document using the approved template (TE-IMS-FRM-SYS-001).</h2>

<h2 class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">2. Review — Draft is sent to the relevant Department Head / Process Owner for technical and operational review.</h2>

<h2 class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">3. Approval — Document is sent to the IMS Project Lead for code assignment, then final approval by the authorised manager (e.g., HSE Manager for HSE documents, CEO for Policies).</h2>

<h2 class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">4. Issuance — Document Controller adds the document to the Master Register and distributes it via email, shared drive, and printed copies where required.</h2>

<h2 id="s7" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">7. DISTRIBUTION &amp; ACCESS</h2>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>All controlled documents are stored in the central IMS repository (shared drive or cloud folder).</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Only current revisions are accessible to users.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Printed copies must be clearly marked “Controlled Copy” and carry the revision date.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Obsolete printed copies must be destroyed or clearly marked “OBSOLETE – Do Not Use”.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s8" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">8. REVISION &amp; CHANGE CONTROL</h2>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Any change requires a new revision number (Rev01, Rev02, etc.).</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Changes are tracked in the Revision History section of each document.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Affected users are notified of revisions via email or Toolbox Talk.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Old revisions are archived electronically — never deleted.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s9" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">9. STORAGE &amp; RETRIEVAL</h2>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Electronic master copies are stored in a secure, backed-up location.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Records are retained for minimum periods (e.g., 5 years for training records, 3 years for incident reports).</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Disposal of records follows NCEC / MHRSD retention rules and applicable Saudi regulations.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s10" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">10. MONITORING &amp; REVIEW</h2>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Monthly check: percentage of documents current, percentage of overdue reviews.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Annual audit of document control effectiveness by the IMS Project Lead.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Results reported and discussed in Management Review Meetings.</span></li>
</ul>

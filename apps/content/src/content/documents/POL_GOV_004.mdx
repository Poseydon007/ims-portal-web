<h2 id="s1" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">1. Purpose and Scope</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #374151;">This Policy establishes the requirements for a drug and alcohol free workplace at True East Mining Company (TEMC). It applies to all employees, contractors, subcontractors, visitors, and any other person present at any TEMC workplace, site, vehicle, or facility in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;">Mining and drilling operations involve heavy machinery, explosives, elevated structures, confined spaces, and high-energy systems. The presence of any person under the influence of drugs or alcohol creates an unacceptable risk of serious injury or death to themselves and others. This Policy is non-negotiable and will be enforced without exception.</p>

<h2 id="s2" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">2. Policy Statement</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #374151;">TEMC is committed to maintaining a workplace that is completely free from the use, possession, distribution, or influence of alcohol, illegal drugs, and any substance that impairs cognitive or physical function. This commitment is grounded in our duty of care to all personnel, our obligations under Saudi law, and our IMS standards.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;">TEMC fully supports the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia's position on alcohol and narcotics. Alcohol is prohibited in the Kingdom, and TEMC will not tolerate any attempt to introduce, possess, or consume alcohol on any TEMC site or in any TEMC vehicle or accommodation.</p>

<h2 id="s4" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">4. Testing</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #374151;">TEMC reserves the right to conduct drug and alcohol testing in the following circumstances:</p>

<ul class="list-none pl-0 space-y-2 mb-3">
  <li class="flex gap-2 text-sm" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28;">•</span><span><strong>Pre-employment</strong> — as a condition of offer for all positions involving safety-critical tasks.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 text-sm" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28;">•</span><span><strong>Post-incident</strong> — following any workplace accident, near miss, or dangerous occurrence where impairment may have been a contributing factor.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 text-sm" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28;">•</span><span><strong>Reasonable cause</strong> — when a supervisor has objective grounds to believe a person is impaired (e.g., slurred speech, unsteady movement, odour, erratic behaviour).</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 text-sm" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28;">•</span><span><strong>Random</strong> — unannounced random testing may be conducted at any TEMC site at the discretion of the HSE Manager.</span></li>
</ul>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;">Refusal to submit to a lawfully requested test will be treated as a positive result and will result in immediate removal from site and disciplinary action.</p>

<h2 id="s5" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">5. Consequences of Violation</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #374151;">Any person found to be in violation of this Policy will be removed from the TEMC site immediately. For employees, violations will result in disciplinary action up to and including summary dismissal, in accordance with the Saudi Labour Law and TEMC's disciplinary procedures. For contractors and subcontractors, violations will result in immediate removal from site and notification to the contracting company; repeat violations may result in contract termination.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;">Where a violation constitutes a criminal offence under Saudi law — including possession or distribution of narcotics — TEMC will report the matter to the competent Saudi authorities without exception.</p>

<h2 id="s6" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">6. Support and Rehabilitation</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;">TEMC recognises that substance dependency is a health condition. Any employee who voluntarily discloses a dependency issue before a violation occurs, and who actively participates in a recognised rehabilitation programme, will be treated with confidentiality and supported through an agreed return-to-work plan. Voluntary disclosure does not exempt an employee from fitness-for-duty requirements or from the prohibition on working under the influence.</p>

<h2 id="s7" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">7. Prescription Medication Disclosure</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;">Employees taking prescription medication that may affect their ability to safely perform their duties must disclose this to the HSE Manager before commencing work. The HSE Manager, in consultation with the employee and where necessary a medical professional, will determine appropriate work restrictions or temporary redeployment. Disclosures are treated in strict confidence.</p>

<h2 id="s8" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">8. Training and Awareness</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;">All employees and relevant contractors receive drug and alcohol awareness training at induction. Supervisors receive additional training on recognising signs of impairment and on the correct procedure for requesting a test. Training completion is recorded in the Training and Competence Matrix (REG-TRN-001).</p>

<h2 id="s9" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">9. Governance and Review</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;">This Policy is owned by the Managing Director. The HSE Manager is responsible for day-to-day implementation, testing coordination, and record maintenance. Policy effectiveness is reviewed at the annual Management Review (PROC-SYS-002) and updated whenever there is a material change in applicable law, site risk profile, or operational structure.</p>

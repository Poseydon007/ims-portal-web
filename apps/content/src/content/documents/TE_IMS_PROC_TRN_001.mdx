<h2 id="s1" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">1. Purpose</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This procedure establishes a systematic process at True East Mining Company (TEMC) for defining job requirement profiles, setting competency levels, identifying training needs, delivering and verifying training, and providing the infrastructure and resources needed for operational excellence and safety. It supports ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, and ISO 45001:2018 (Clauses 7.1 Resources, 7.2 Competence, 7.3 Awareness) and contributes to TEMC's Saudization objectives under MHRSD requirements.</p>

<h2 id="s2" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">2. Scope</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This procedure applies to all TEMC employees and long-term contractors across drilling, maintenance, HSE, logistics, procurement, HR, finance, and administration functions. It covers:</p>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Job profiling and competency requirements.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Induction and onboarding.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Identification of training needs (TNA).</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Planning, approval, and execution of training.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Trainee evaluation and certification.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Resource and infrastructure provision.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Review, monitoring, and continual improvement.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s3" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">3. Definitions</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Training Needs Analysis (TNA) — a systematic process to identify gaps between current and required competencies for each role.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Competence — the demonstrated ability to apply knowledge and skills to achieve intended results safely and effectively.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>OJT (On-the-Job Training) — practical hands-on training conducted in the actual work environment under a competent person.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Job Requirement Profile (JRP) — a controlled document defining job title, reporting line, responsibilities, minimum qualifications, experience, and required competencies.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Saudization — KSA Nitaqat / MHRSD programme requiring nationalisation of the workforce.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Certification — formal evidence that a trainee has completed training and been verified competent.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s5" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">5. Job Profiling and Competency Requirements</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Process Owners document a formal Job Requirement Profile (FRM-HR-001) for every position.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Each JRP includes: job title, reporting line, primary responsibilities, minimum qualifications, minimum experience, required certifications (technical and HSE), language requirements, and required competency levels.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>The HR Manager consolidates JRPs; the MD reviews and approves them annually.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>HR matches all new hires to the approved JRP for the position; CVs and certificates are retained in the employee file.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s6" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">6. Induction and Onboarding</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Every new employee attends the TEMC Induction and Orientation Programme on or before the first day of work.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>The induction covers: company overview, IMS policy and objectives, HSE rules, emergency procedures, site rules, code of conduct, HR policies, and role-specific orientation.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Site-based personnel additionally receive site induction (rig, camp, or yard specific) by the Site HSE Officer before starting work.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>On completion of induction, the Process Owner evaluates the new employee on the Trainee Evaluation Report (FRM-HR-003) and sends a copy to HR for the employee file.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s7" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">7. Training Needs Analysis (TNA)</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">The HR Manager, with Department Heads, conducts TNA annually and when triggered by new equipment, new methods, changed roles, incidents, or audit findings. Training is classified as:</p>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Strategic Training — leadership, project management, commercial, and ISO awareness — supports company-level capability.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Functional Training — role-specific technical training (drilling, geology, mud engineering, maintenance, HSE, logistics) — supports operational performance.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Vocational Training — skills training that supports technology change or career progression — supports Saudization objectives.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>On-the-Job Training (OJT) — supervised hands-on training on new machinery, methods, or procedures — supports safe execution.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Mandatory Compliance Training — HSE and regulatory: LOTO, working at height, confined space, first aid, fire warden, hazardous materials, emergency response, defensive driving — tracked with expiry dates.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s8" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">8. Approval and Execution of Training</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>8.1 Request — HR or a Department Head raises a Training Request (FRM-HR-002) defining the training, target audience, objective, provider, cost, and date.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>8.2 Planning — HR consolidates requests into the Annual Training Plan, balancing schedule, budget, operational release, and Saudization priorities.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>8.3 Approval — the MD approves the Annual Training Plan. Unplanned training during the year requires MD approval on the Training Request.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>8.4 Execution — HR books the provider, notifies the department, posts the schedule on the department noticeboard, provides training materials, tracks attendance in the Training Attendance Register (FRM-TRN-001), and files all records.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>8.5 OJT — is executed by a nominated competent mentor, documented on the OJT record, and signed off when the trainee has demonstrated the required competency.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s9" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">9. Trainee Evaluation, Certification, and Competence Verification</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>At the end of each training event, the trainee is assessed by the trainer (written test, practical, or both).</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Passing trainees receive a Training Certificate; failing trainees are re-trained and re-assessed.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>The Process Owner verifies on-the-job application of the new skills over a defined period and records the verification in the Training and Competence Matrix (REG-TRN-001).</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>HSE-critical certifications (first aid, LOTO, working at height, confined space, defensive driving) are tracked with expiry dates; refresher training is booked before expiry.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Training records are retained for the duration of employment plus five (5) years.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s10" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">10. Resources and Infrastructure (ISO 7.1)</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>At the start of each year, Process Owners identify and justify the resources needed to achieve quality, HSE, and operational objectives.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Requirements include: manpower, equipment and tools, IT infrastructure (computers, software, communications), workspace, PPE, training facilities, and welfare.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Process Owners submit a Resource Matrix (FRM-HR-004) to the MD; the MD allocates resources through the annual budget and CAPEX plan.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Mid-year requests are raised through the Procurement and CAPEX approval route under PROC-SCM-001.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s11" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">11. Monitoring and Continual Improvement</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>The HR Manager tracks KPIs (section 12) monthly and reports at the Management Review.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Post-training effectiveness is measured through: trainer evaluation, trainee feedback, Process Owner verification on the job, and incident/audit data.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>The annual training plan is reviewed and updated based on effectiveness data and any changes in strategy, risk, or regulation.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Ineffective training is flagged as a non-conformity and processed under PROC-SYS-008 Corrective Action.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Saudization progress is reported quarterly to the MD and aligned to MHRSD Nitaqat requirements.</span></li>
</ul>

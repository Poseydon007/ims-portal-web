<h2 id="s1" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">1. Purpose</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This procedure establishes a systematic process for planning, scheduling, executing, recording, and continually improving maintenance activities on all True East Mining Company (TEMC) assets — drill rigs, generators, vehicles, pumps, compressors, workshop equipment, and camp facilities. The objective is to achieve safe working conditions during maintenance, high equipment availability and reliability, prevention of breakdowns and incidents, and compliance with ISO 9001:2015 (Clause 8.5), ISO 45001:2018 (Clause 8.1.2), ISO 14001:2015 (Clause 8.1), and KSA regulatory requirements (MHRSD, NCEC).</p>

<h2 id="s2" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">2. Scope</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This procedure applies to all maintenance activities — preventive, predictive, corrective, and emergency — on company-owned or rented assets used in drilling, exploration, transport, camp, and support operations in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Major capital repairs and third-party specialised maintenance (e.g. rig engine rebuilds) are excluded from routine maintenance and managed under Procurement / Contractor Management.</p>

<h2 id="s3" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">3. Definitions</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Preventive Maintenance (PM) — scheduled maintenance performed at fixed intervals (time, hours, km) to prevent failure.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Predictive Maintenance (PdM) — condition-based maintenance triggered by monitoring (vibration, oil analysis, thermography).</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Corrective Maintenance (CM) — work performed to restore a failed item or correct a defect.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Emergency Maintenance (EM) — immediate action to restore safety or critical operations after an unplanned failure.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Critical Equipment — equipment whose failure would stop production, create a safety hazard, or cause environmental impact.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Work Order — the controlled document authorising and recording a maintenance task.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>LOTO — lockout / tagout — isolation of energy sources before work begins (per SOP-HSE-003).</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>MTBF — Mean Time Between Failures — reliability KPI.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>OEE — Overall Equipment Effectiveness — availability × performance × quality.</span></li>
</ul>

<h3 class="text-sm font-bold mt-5 mb-2" style="color: #081C2E;">5.1 Mandatory Safety Controls</h3>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>LOTO mandatory for all energy isolation — electrical, hydraulic, pneumatic, mechanical (SOP-HSE-003).</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Machine guarding — all guards in place and functional before release to service (SOP-MAINT-001).</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>PPE — hard hat, safety glasses, gloves, overalls, steel-toe boots; task-specific additions (face shield, hearing, respiratory).</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Hot Work Permit required for welding, grinding, cutting (SOP-HSE-004).</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Confined Space Permit required for tank / pump / pit entry (SOP-HSE-002).</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Spill prevention — drip trays, spill kits, bunding during oil and fuel work (PROC-HSE-015).</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Working at Height controls apply to elevated maintenance (PLN-HSE-003).</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s6" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">6. Planning and Scheduling</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Annual maintenance plan prepared at year start using OEM manuals, historical data, operating hours, and risk.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Schedule updated monthly based on usage, defects, and seasonal factors (e.g. more cooling-system checks in summer).</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Critical equipment scheduled during low-activity periods where possible (weekend shutdowns).</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Spare parts and consumables pre-staged — Procurement lead time built into the schedule.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Planned downtime coordinated with Operations and communicated at daily and weekly meetings.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s7" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">7. Execution and Documentation</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Work Order — every task uses TE-IMS-FRM-MAINT-002 — describes task, parts, tools, JHA reference, safety controls.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Pre-job brief — toolbox talk at the start of each task covering hazards, isolation, permits, PPE, and emergency procedures.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Records — date, time, technician, work done, parts used, issues found, time taken — recorded on the Work Order and in the Maintenance Log (TE-IMS-REG-MAINT-001).</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Post-work test — function test and re-commissioning before release to operations. Signed off by competent person.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Housekeeping — work area left clean; waste segregated; tools returned; guards reinstated.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s8" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">8. Breakdown and Emergency Response</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Operator reports defect via radio and defect card immediately.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Equipment isolated and tagged Out of Service if unsafe to continue.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Maintenance Supervisor classifies severity (Safety-Critical / Production-Critical / Non-Critical) and assigns response priority.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Stop Work Authority is exercised when safety controls are missing or defeated.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Post-event review for any breakdown affecting safety or critical production — root cause analysis per PROC-SYS-005.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s9" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">9. Verification and Effectiveness</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Monthly review of maintenance performance — downtime, breakdowns, compliance, spares consumption, cost.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Post-maintenance function test on critical equipment (rigs, generators, winches) before return to service.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Annual management review of the maintenance programme — budget, KPIs, changes.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Trend analysis identifies repeat failures and drives design, training, or procedure changes.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s1" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">1. PURPOSE</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This procedure establishes a controlled, systematic process for identifying, assessing, approving, implementing, and verifying changes that could affect the Integrated Management System (IMS), product quality, health &amp; safety, environmental performance, or operational risks at True East Mining Company. It ensures that all changes are evaluated for potential impacts and managed in a way that maintains IMS integrity and compliance with:</p>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>ISO 9001:2015 (clause 8.5.6) — Control of Changes.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>ISO 14001:2015 (clause 8.1) — Operational Planning and Control.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>ISO 45001:2018 (clause 8.1.3) — Management of Change.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s2" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">2. SCOPE</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This procedure applies to all changes that could impact the IMS or operations, including but not limited to:</p>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Changes to processes, procedures, or SOPs.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Changes to equipment, machinery, or rig configuration.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Changes to personnel, roles, or competency requirements.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Changes to suppliers, contractors, or materials.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Changes to legal or regulatory requirements, or client specifications.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Organisational changes (e.g., new site, restructuring).</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Changes to objectives, targets, or risk controls.</span></li>
</ul>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">Exclusions:</p>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Minor cosmetic changes (e.g., typo corrections without operational or risk impact) do not require formal change management.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s3" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">3. RESPONSIBILITIES</h2>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Change Initiator (any employee / supervisor) — Identify the need for change, complete the Change Request Form, and carry out the initial impact assessment.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>IMS Project Lead / Document Controller — Assign change code, track progress, update the Master Document Register, and close the change.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Relevant Department Head — Review and approve changes in their area; ensure risk assessment is thorough and complete.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>HSE Manager — Review all changes with HSE impact (safety, environment, emergency response).</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>CEO / Managing Director — Approve high-risk or strategic changes (e.g., major process change, new rig type, new contract scope).</span></li>
</ul>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">Changes are categorised by potential impact and approved at the appropriate authority level:</p>

<h2 id="s5" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">5. PROCEDURE STEPS</h2>

<h2 class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">1. Initiate Change — Any employee identifies a need for change (improvement, regulatory requirement, incident lesson learned). The Change Initiator completes the Change Request Form (TE-IMS-FRM-SYS-001).</h2>

<h2 class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">2. Assess Impact — Evaluator (usually initiator and supervisor) assesses potential impact on the following areas:</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">› Safety — injury risk, emergency response.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">› Environment — spill, waste, rehabilitation.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">› Quality — product or service delivery.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">› Operations — downtime, cost.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">› Legal / compliance — MHRSD, NCEC, client requirements.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">Use the Risk Assessment Procedure (TE-IMS-PROC-HSE-003) if impact is medium or high.</p>

<h2 class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">3. Risk &amp; Approval — Change is routed to the appropriate approval authority based on impact level (see Section 4). Approval is documented on the Change Request Form.</h2>

<h2 class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">4. Implement Change — Update affected documents (procedures, SOPs, forms) with new revision. Notify affected personnel via email, Toolbox Talk, or formal training. Implement communication or training as required.</h2>

<h2 class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">5. Verify Effectiveness — Monitor post-change performance (e.g., no new incidents, objectives still met). Verify effectiveness in the next Management Review Meeting, then close the change in the Master Document Register.</h2>

<h2 id="s6" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">6. EMERGENCY CHANGES</h2>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>In urgent cases (imminent safety risk), a change may be implemented immediately with verbal approval from the HSE Manager or Site Manager.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>A formal Change Request Form and documented approval must follow within 24 hours of the emergency change.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>All emergency changes are reported at the next Management Review Meeting.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s7" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">7. RECORDS &amp; RETENTION</h2>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>All Change Request Forms and approvals retained for a minimum of 5 years.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Stored in a secure, controlled location within the IMS repository.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Tracked in the Master Document Register (TE-IMS-REG-SYS-001).</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s8" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">8. MONITORING &amp; CONTINUAL IMPROVEMENT</h2>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Track number of changes raised, percentage approved, and percentage effective (KPI reported under Pillar 8 – Continual Improvement).</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Review effectiveness of this procedure annually during the Management Review.</span></li>
</ul>

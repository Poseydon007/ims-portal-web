            <div class="mb-4 pb-3 mt-8" style="border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">
              <h2 id="s1" class="text-lg font-bold" style="color: #081C2E;">1. Purpose</h2>
            </div>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This SOP sets the minimum safe-driving standard for every driver operating a light vehicle, 4x4, or heavy vehicle on behalf of True East Mining Company (TEMC) anywhere in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. It is the shop-floor companion to PROC-LOG-001 Company Vehicle Usage and PROC-LOG-002 Journey Management, and is written to give drivers, supervisors, and passengers a single, clear rule set.</p>

            <div class="mb-4 pb-3 mt-8" style="border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">
              <h2 id="s2" class="text-lg font-bold" style="color: #081C2E;">2. Scope</h2>
            </div>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This SOP applies to all TEMC-owned, leased, rented, and contractor vehicles — light vehicles, 4x4s, heavy trucks, and specialised fleet — on TEMC sites, client sites, public roads, and desert tracks. It binds drivers, passengers, contractors, and visitors.</p>

            <div class="mb-4 pb-3 mt-8" style="border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">
              <h2 id="s3" class="text-lg font-bold" style="color: #081C2E;">3. Driver Qualification and Authorisation</h2>
            </div>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Licence — valid KSA driving licence of the correct class for the vehicle (light / heavy / specialised).</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Clean record — no major traffic violations in the past 3 years, verified via MVR where available.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Fitness — medically and mentally fit; vision and hearing adequate; no disqualifying medication.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Training — TEMC Defensive Driving (initial + 2-year refresher); Desert / Off-Road Driving course before any off-road task.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Site induction — passed company road test and site induction covering local hazards — dust, heat, wildlife, heavy machinery interaction.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Authorisation — recorded in the IMS Driver Register; authorisation letter held in personnel file.</p>

            <div class="mb-4 pb-3 mt-8" style="border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">
              <h2 id="s4" class="text-lg font-bold" style="color: #081C2E;">4. Driver Performance Expectations</h2>
            </div>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Comply strictly with Saudi traffic laws, client speed limits, road signage, and site-specific protocols.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Drive defensively at all times — safe following distance, anticipate hazards, avoid distractions, use mirrors and blind-spot checks.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Maintain full control and accountability of the vehicle — daily pre-use inspection, report defects, log fuel and kilometres.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Zero tolerance for impairment — alcohol, drugs, severe fatigue, impairing medication.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Attend all refresher training and toolbox talks.</p>

            <div class="mb-4 pb-3 mt-8" style="border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">
              <h2 id="s5" class="text-lg font-bold" style="color: #081C2E;">5. General Rules</h2>
            </div>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Seatbelts — mandatory for driver and every passenger. Passenger limit = number of functional seatbelts. No riding in pickup beds or cargo areas.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Pre-use inspection — daily walk-around — tyres, lights, fluids, brakes, leaks, wipers, horn, mirrors, first aid, extinguisher.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Mobile phones — prohibited while driving. Pull over safely before making or receiving a call. Hands-free only where specifically authorised.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Speed — obey KSA and client speed limits; reduce for poor conditions, dust, night, wet, or fatigue.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Parking — designated areas only; reverse parking mandatory.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Uphill parking: wheels turned to curb, handbrake ON, gear in Reverse (manual) or Park (auto).</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Downhill parking: wheels turned away from curb, handbrake ON, gear in 1st (manual) or Park (auto).</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Idling / keys — never leave AC running with occupants inside an unattended vehicle; never leave keys in the ignition.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Behaviour — no horseplay, no unauthorised passengers, no shortcuts through hazard areas.</p>

            <div class="mb-4 pb-3 mt-8" style="border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">
              <h2 id="s6" class="text-lg font-bold" style="color: #081C2E;">6. Prohibited Cabin Items (Heat Risk)</h2>
            </div>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">KSA cabin temperatures can exceed 70°C. The following must be removed after use:</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Lighters, matches, and flammable aerosols.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Body sprays and pressurised cans.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Pressurised or frozen water bottles.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Battery chargers, power banks, lithium batteries.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Mobile phones and sunglasses left on the dash.</p>

            <div class="mb-4 pb-3 mt-8" style="border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">
              <h2 id="s7" class="text-lg font-bold" style="color: #081C2E;">7. Adverse Conditions</h2>
            </div>

            <div class="mb-4 pb-3 mt-8" style="border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">
              <h2 id="s8" class="text-lg font-bold" style="color: #081C2E;">8. Desert and Off-Road Driving</h2>
            </div>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Vehicle — approved 4x4 only; no light vehicle off road.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Tyre pressure — lower to 12–15 psi on soft sand to increase traction. Re-inflate immediately on return to hard road.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Convoy — minimum 2 vehicles for remote travel; never alone off established tracks.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Technique — stick to established tracks; maintain smooth momentum; avoid sudden braking or sharp turns in soft sand.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Recovery gear — kinetic rope, sand boards, shovel, spare tyre, jack, compressor, GPS, satellite phone, water.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Communications — inform base of route and ETA; Journey Management Plan mandatory for off-road travel.</p>

            <div class="mb-4 pb-3 mt-8" style="border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">
              <h2 id="s9" class="text-lg font-bold" style="color: #081C2E;">9. Vehicle Inspection and Maintenance</h2>
            </div>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Daily: pre-use inspection using FRM-LOG-002 before first movement.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Monthly: detailed inspection by Fleet / Maintenance — tyres, brakes, lights, fluids, battery, belts, hoses.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Servicing: per manufacturer schedule; recorded in REG-MAINT-001.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• On-board safety equipment: First Aid kit, fire extinguisher (≥2 kg ABC), reflective triangles, spare tyre in good condition, heavy-duty tools, high-visibility vests.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Tag any defective vehicle Out of Service immediately.</p>

            <div class="mb-4 pb-3 mt-8" style="border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">
              <h2 id="s10" class="text-lg font-bold" style="color: #081C2E;">10. Incident and Accident Reporting</h2>
            </div>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• All incidents and near-misses reported immediately to Supervisor and HSE via Flash Notification (FRM-HSE-005).</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• For collisions involving injury, third party, or significant damage, contact Najm 920000560 (accident reporting) and Traffic Police 993 / 999 as applicable.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Secure the scene — deploy reflective triangles, activate hazards, wear high-visibility vests, keep occupants away from traffic.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Do not move the vehicle unless there is an immediate secondary threat or police clearance is given.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Medical emergency: call Saudi Red Crescent Ambulance 997 or Unified 911.</p>

            <div class="mb-4 pb-3 mt-8" style="border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">
              <h2 id="s11" class="text-lg font-bold" style="color: #081C2E;">11. Monitoring and Continual Improvement</h2>
            </div>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• IVMS / GPS telemetry continuously monitors location, speed, harsh events, and driver behaviour.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Quarterly audits of vehicles, authorised drivers, and inspection records.</p>

            <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Annual review of this SOP; interim review after any serious incident.</p>

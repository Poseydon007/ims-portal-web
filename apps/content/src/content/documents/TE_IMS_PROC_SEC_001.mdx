<h2 id="s1" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">1. Purpose</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This procedure establishes the minimum standards for physical security, access control, visitor management, asset protection, and security-incident response at all True East Mining Company (TEMC) offices, drill sites, camps, yards, and fuel/chemical storage areas in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. It protects personnel, assets, information, and operations from unauthorised access, theft, sabotage, and hostile events, and supports ISO 45001:2018 (Clauses 8.1.2, 8.2) and KSA regulatory requirements.</p>

<h2 id="s2" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">2. Scope</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This procedure applies to all TEMC employees, contractors, visitors, suppliers, and security service providers at every TEMC-controlled location — head office, project offices, drilling camps, exploration sites, workshops, fuel storage, core yards, and temporary project locations.</p>

<h2 id="s3" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">3. Definitions</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Security — the state of being free from danger, threat, unauthorised access, or loss.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Access Control — system of approvals, credentials, and physical controls managing who enters and leaves a site.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Visitor — any non-TEMC person on site — client, contractor, supplier, consultant, auditor, government inspector, guest.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Security Incident — any breach, attempted breach, theft, vandalism, trespass, threat, or suspicious activity.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Restricted Area — designated area with additional access controls — fuel storage, explosives magazine, core library, server room, finance office.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Security Provider — approved third-party contractor providing guards, patrols, alarm, or monitoring services.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s5" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">5. Access Control</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>All TEMC sites are perimeter-controlled with manned gate, barrier, fence, or designated entry point.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Employees display a valid company ID at all times.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Entry and exit of all personnel and vehicles logged in the Gate Register (FRM-SEC-001).</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Contractor access requires valid TEMC site induction, ID, and a sponsor.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Restricted areas (fuel storage, core library, server room, finance office) require additional authorisation by the Site Manager or function head.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Out-of-hours access requires pre-authorisation from the Site Manager or SSE.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s6" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">6. Visitor Management</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Visitors park in the designated external parking area.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Guard obtains visitor name, organisation, and host. Telephones the host for permission to admit.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>On approval, visitor signs the Visitor Register (FRM-SEC-002) — name, company, ID / Iqama, purpose, entry time — and surrenders ID for the duration of the visit.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Visitor receives a Visitor Badge. The host escorts the visitor at all times on site.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>On departure, ID is returned, badge collected, and exit time recorded.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Out-of-hours visits are not permitted unless pre-arranged in writing. In disputes, the guard contacts the designated out-of-hours number posted in the Guard House.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s7" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">7. Vehicle and Material Movement</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Inbound deliveries: guard verifies driver, vehicle, and delivery documentation. Notifies the Warehouse, Logistics, or Maintenance supervisor. Directs the driver to the correct unloading bay.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Outbound goods: guard checks and stamps the Gate Pass (FRM-SEC-003). Only signed and authorised Gate Passes permit exit of company property.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Personal vehicles and bags may be subject to inspection at the gate in accordance with TEMC policy and KSA law.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Fuel tankers and chemical deliveries follow SOP-MAINT-002 and PROC-SCM-001.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s8" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">8. Security Patrols</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Guards conduct perimeter and internal patrols per the roster issued by the Security Coordinator.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Patrol findings recorded on the Daily Security Patrol Report (FRM-SEC-004).</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Any suspicious activity, breach, damage, or unauthorised person is reported immediately to the Security Coordinator / Site Manager.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Patrol timing is varied to avoid predictability; check-in points used on remote sites.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s9" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">9. Key Control</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>All operational and restricted-area keys are held in a locked Key Cabinet in the Guard House or Security Office.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Keys are issued only to authorised personnel, recorded in the Key Register (FRM-SEC-005) with signature on issue and return.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>The Key Cabinet is kept locked whenever not in active use.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Lost keys are reported immediately; affected locks rekeyed or replaced.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s10" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">10. Asset and Information Protection</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Company equipment removed from site only with an approved Gate Pass (FRM-SEC-003).</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Confidential documents (contracts, financials, exploration data, employee files) stored in locked cabinets or controlled digital systems.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>IT access, passwords, and data handled under the IT / Information Security procedure.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Photography and mobile phone use in restricted or client areas only with prior authorisation.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s11" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">11. Security Incident Response</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Any security event — intrusion, theft, vandalism, threat, suspicious activity, trespass — is reported immediately to Security Coordinator and Site Manager.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Personnel do not personally confront intruders. Observe, report, preserve evidence.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>For violent or armed events, dial Saudi Police 999 and Civil Defense 998 immediately. Secure and evacuate if required.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>All incidents logged in the Security Incident Register (REG-SEC-001) and investigated per PROC-HSE-006 Incident Investigation where applicable.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Major security events escalate to PLN-HSE-001 Major Emergency Preparedness Plan.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s12" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">12. Training and Awareness</h2>

  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Security guards trained in access control, visitor management, patrol, incident response, and KSA legal limits on the use of force. Certification maintained.</span></li>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">

</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Annual refresher or immediately after any security incident.</span></li>
</ul>

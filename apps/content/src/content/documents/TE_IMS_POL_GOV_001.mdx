<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">1. Policy Statement</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">True East Mining Company (TEMC) is committed to the highest standards of Health, Safety, and Environmental performance in every aspect of its mineral exploration, drilling, and related operations in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">2. Our Core Beliefs</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Incident Prevention — all incidents are preventable.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Zero Harm — zero harm to people is achievable.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Environmental Protection — environmental protection is non-negotiable.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Quality Excellence — quality excellence is essential for long-term success.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">3. Our Strategic Commitments</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">To deliver these beliefs TEMC will:</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Safe Working Conditions — provide safe and healthy working conditions to prevent work-related injury and ill health — including fatigue, heat stress, and musculoskeletal disorders — appropriate to the organisation's size and context.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Hazard Elimination — eliminate hazards and reduce HSE risks using the Hierarchy of Controls: Elimination, Substitution, Engineering, Administrative, and PPE as a last resort.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Legal Compliance — comply with all applicable legal requirements, including MHRSD regulations, NCEC / MEWA environmental standards, Saudi Mining Investment Law, and client-specific obligations such as Ma'aden Contractor HSE Standards.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Sustainability — prevent pollution, minimise waste, and promote sustainable resource use across all drilling, exploration, maintenance, and transport activities.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Worker Consultation — involve workers and representatives in the development, planning, and continual improvement of the IMS.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Objectives and Targets — establish and regularly review measurable HSE objectives and targets across all relevant functions.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Resource Provision — provide necessary training, equipment, competent personnel, and infrastructure to achieve our goals.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Safety Culture — empower all personnel and contractors with Stop Work Authority, allowing them to halt unsafe work without fear of reprisal.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Open Communication — communicate performance and policy openly with employees, contractors, clients, and regulators.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Continual Improvement — enhance the IMS through regular audits, management reviews, and incident investigations.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">4. Application</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This policy applies to all True East Mining operations, personnel, contractors, and visitors. It is reviewed annually or following significant operational, regulatory, or strategic changes.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">5. References</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, ISO 45001:2018.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• KSA Mining Investment Law.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• MHRSD — Occupational Safety and Health Regulations.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• NCEC / MEWA — Environmental Regulations.</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">• Ma'aden Contractor HSE Standards (where applicable).</p>

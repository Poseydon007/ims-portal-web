<h2 id="s1" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">1. PURPOSE</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This procedure establishes a systematic, planned process for conducting internal audits of the Integrated Management System (IMS) at True East Mining Company to verify:</p>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Conformity to planned arrangements (procedures, SOPs, policies).</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Effective implementation and maintenance of the IMS.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Continual improvement of the IMS.</span></li>
</ul>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">It ensures compliance with:</p>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>ISO 9001:2015 (clause 9.2) — Internal Audit.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>ISO 14001:2015 (clause 9.2) — Internal Audit.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>ISO 45001:2018 (clause 9.2) — Internal Audit.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s2" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">2. SCOPE</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This procedure applies to all internal audits of the IMS, including:</p>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Scheduled audits under the annual audit program.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Unscheduled audits (e.g., after major incidents, significant changes, or regulatory findings).</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Audits of all pillars, processes, sites, rigs, and functions within the organisation.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s3" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">3. RESPONSIBILITIES</h2>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>IMS Project Lead / Management Representative — Plan and coordinate the audit program, select auditors, issue audit reports, and track corrective actions.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>CEO / Managing Director — Approve the annual audit program and review major findings in Management Review Meetings.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Internal Auditors — Conduct audits objectively and impartially; document findings; verify corrective actions.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Auditees (Department Heads / Process Owners) — Provide access to records and personnel; implement corrective actions; verify effectiveness.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s4" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">4. AUDITOR SELECTION &amp; COMPETENCE</h2>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Auditors must be independent of the area being audited.</span></li>
</ul>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">Competence requirements:</p>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Training in ISO 9001 / 14001 / 45001 auditing principles.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Understanding of mining and drilling operations.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Knowledge of relevant legal requirements (MHRSD, NCEC, client standards).</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Auditor competence records are maintained in the Training &amp; Competence Matrix (TE-IMS-REG-TRN-001).</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s5" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">5. AUDIT PROGRAM</h2>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Annual Audit Plan — Prepared by the IMS Project Lead and approved by the CEO at the start of each calendar year.</span></li>
</ul>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">Audit frequency by process category:</p>

<div class="overflow-x-auto mb-6 rounded border" style="border-color: #dde3ec;">
  <table class="w-full text-sm border-collapse">
    <thead style="background-color: #081C2E;">
      <tr>
        <th class="px-3 py-2 text-left text-xs font-bold uppercase tracking-wider" style="color: #ffffff;">Process Category</th>
        <th class="px-3 py-2 text-left text-xs font-bold uppercase tracking-wider" style="color: #ffffff;">Audit Frequency</th>
      </tr>
    </thead>
    <tbody>
      <tr style="background-color: rgba(8,28,46,0.03);">
        <td class="px-3 py-2 text-xs" style="color: #081C2E;">High-risk processes (drilling, emergency response, LOTO, hot work, refuelling)</td>
        <td class="px-3 py-2 text-xs" style="color: #081C2E;">At least once per year</td>
      </tr>
      <tr>
        <td class="px-3 py-2 text-xs" style="color: #081C2E;">Other processes</td>
        <td class="px-3 py-2 text-xs" style="color: #081C2E;">At least once every 2 years</td>
      </tr>
      <tr style="background-color: rgba(8,28,46,0.03);">
        <td class="px-3 py-2 text-xs" style="color: #081C2E;">Unscheduled audits</td>
        <td class="px-3 py-2 text-xs" style="color: #081C2E;">Triggered by incidents, major changes, or non-conformities</td>
      </tr>
    </tbody>
  </table>
</div>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">Audit criteria:</p>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Importance and risk of the process.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Results of previous audits.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Changes affecting the IMS.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Client and regulatory feedback.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s6" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">6. AUDIT EXECUTION STEPS</h2>

<h2 class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">1. Plan the Audit — Assign auditor(s), define scope, and set schedule. Notify the auditee at least 2 weeks in advance.</h2>

<h2 class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">2. Prepare — Review relevant documents (procedures, previous audit reports, risk register, objectives and targets).</h2>

<h2 class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">3. Conduct Opening Meeting — Explain purpose, scope, and method of the audit; confirm schedule and availability.</h2>

<h2 class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">4. Collect Evidence — Gather evidence through interviews, observations, document review, and site walk-throughs.</h2>

<h2 class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">5. Identify Findings — Document conformities, non-conformities (classified as minor or major), and opportunities for improvement.</h2>

<h2 class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">6. Closing Meeting — Present findings to the auditee and obtain agreement on identified non-conformities.</h2>

<h2 id="s7" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">7. Report — Issue the Audit Report (TE-IMS-FRM-SYS-001) within 7 days of the closing meeting.</h2>

<h2 class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">7. CORRECTIVE ACTIONS &amp; FOLLOW-UP</h2>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Auditee prepares a corrective action plan (root cause, actions, owner, deadline).</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Plan is submitted to the IMS Project Lead within 14 days of the audit report.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Verification of implementation and effectiveness is carried out via on-site check or document review.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Actions are closed in the Master Document Register. Major non-conformities are escalated to the Management Review Meeting.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s8" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">8. RECORDS &amp; RETENTION</h2>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Audit plans, checklists, reports, and corrective action evidence are retained for a minimum of 5 years.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Stored in a secure, controlled location within the IMS repository.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Tracked in the Master Document Register (TE-IMS-REG-SYS-001).</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s9" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">9. MONITORING &amp; CONTINUAL IMPROVEMENT</h2>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Track KPIs: percentage of audits on time, percentage of actions closed on time, and number of major non-conformities.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Review effectiveness of the audit program in Management Review Meetings.</span></li>
</ul>

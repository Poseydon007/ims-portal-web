<h2 id="s1" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">1. Purpose</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This procedure ensures the safe, efficient, and compliant management of all vehicle journeys undertaken on behalf of True East Mining Company (TEMC) by proactively identifying, assessing, and mitigating road-related risks — driver fatigue, adverse weather, remote terrain, wildlife, and vehicle breakdown. It aligns with ISO 45001:2018, MHRSD traffic and occupational safety regulations, and client fleet standards in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.</p>

<h2 id="s2" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">2. Scope</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This procedure applies to all journeys involving company or contractor vehicles that meet any of the following triggers: distance greater than 50 km, off-road travel, travel to a remote desert site, travel under high-risk weather conditions, travel outside daylight hours, or any journey classified as high-risk by the Journey Coordinator. It covers personnel transport, equipment delivery, sample transport, and general site access in desert and mining environments.</p>

<h2 id="s3" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">3. Definitions</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Journey Management Plan (JMP) — a formal plan on TE-IMS-FRM-LOG-003 that records the route, driver, vehicle, timings, hazards, check-in points, and approvals for a specific journey.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Journey Coordinator — the site role that reviews, approves, and monitors JMPs and tracks drivers through the journey.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Check-In Milestone — a pre-defined point at which the driver contacts the Journey Coordinator to confirm progress and safety.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Remote Area — any location more than 30 km from a permanent base, or without reliable mobile phone coverage.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Fatigue — a state of physical or mental tiredness that reduces alertness and judgment — treated as an impairment.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Convoy — two or more vehicles travelling together for safety on remote or high-risk routes.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s5" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">5. Pre-Trip Planning — Stop and Think</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Necessity — confirm the journey is essential. Can the task be done remotely, combined with another, or deferred?</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Route selection — choose the safest, most direct route. Avoid fatigue hotspots, wadis, sand drifts, and known high-risk zones.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Weather check — confirm official forecast — no sandstorm, flash flood, or extreme heat warning. Cancel or reschedule if forecast is adverse.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Risk evaluation — complete and submit the JMP Form (TE-IMS-FRM-LOG-003) for every journey &gt;50 km or any off-road travel. Formal approval required before departure.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Convoy decision — high-risk routes, long remote travel, or new-to-area drivers travel in convoy of 2 or more vehicles.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s6" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">6. Driver and Vehicle Readiness</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Supervisor confirms driver is well-rested, competent, licensed, and free from alcohol, drugs, or impairing medication.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Driver completes the Daily Vehicle Checklist (TE-IMS-FRM-LOG-002) before departure — tyres, brakes, lights, fluids, fuel, comms, emergency kit.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Minimum provisions on board: 10 litres drinking water per person, shade/tarp, non-perishable food for long journeys, first aid kit, recovery tools.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Vehicle fuel at minimum 3/4 tank at departure for remote travel.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Driver briefed on JMP, check-in schedule, emergency contacts, and alternate route.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s7" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">7. Communication and Monitoring</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Equipment — GPS tracker mandatory; satellite phone mandatory for remote / no-coverage routes; mobile phone and/or two-way radio as backup.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Check-in protocol — driver reports to Journey Coordinator at departure, pre-defined milestones, and arrival.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Active tracking — Journey Coordinator uses GPS telemetry to monitor route adherence, speed, and rest stops in real time.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Overdue trigger — failure to check in within 30 minutes of scheduled milestone activates the site Emergency Response Plan — Journey Coordinator contacts driver, HSE Manager, and if required Civil Defense 998.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s8" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">8. Safe Execution — Proceed Safely</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Rest breaks — mandatory 15-minute rest every 2 hours of continuous driving.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Maximum driving hours — 10 hours in any 24-hour period; maximum 4 hours after a rest break; no driving on ≤ 6 hours sleep.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Night driving — strictly prohibited between sunset and sunrise (19:00–05:00) unless specifically authorised in writing by the HSE Manager — documented approval required with additional controls (convoy, route, comms, rest).</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Camels and livestock — major hazard on KSA roads. Reduce speed in known crossing zones, scan verges, avoid swerving.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Dust storms — if visibility drops below 100 m, pull fully off the road to a safe shoulder, engage hazards, wait until visibility returns.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Flash flood — never drive through flowing water or a wadi. Turn around. Wait for water to recede.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s9" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">9. Emergency Response — Breakdown or Incident</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Pull over to a safe location; deploy hazard lights and reflective warning triangles behind the vehicle.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Secure the scene — high-visibility vests on, keep passengers away from moving traffic.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Contact the Journey Coordinator, Supervisor, or HSE — provide location (GPS), nature of issue, and injuries.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Medical emergency: call 997 (Saudi Red Crescent Ambulance) or 911 (Unified Emergency) immediately.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Desert Survival Rule — stay with the vehicle in remote/desert areas. Do not wander. Shelter in shade. Ration water. Wait for rescue.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Fire: evacuate upwind 50 m; use on-board extinguisher only on small fires that are safe to approach.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Report the event via Flash Notification (TE-IMS-FRM-HSE-005) immediately after safety is established.</span></li>
</ul>

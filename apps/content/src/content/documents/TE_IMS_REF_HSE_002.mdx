<h2 id="s2" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">2. Operating Procedure</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #374151;">Follow these steps when operating a fire extinguisher:</p>

<ul class="list-none pl-0 space-y-2 mb-4">
  <li class="flex gap-2 text-sm" style="color: #374151;"><span class="font-bold shrink-0" style="color: #C49A28;">1.</span><span>Carry or drag the extinguisher to the scene of the fire.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 text-sm" style="color: #374151;"><span class="font-bold shrink-0" style="color: #C49A28;">2.</span><span>Stand approximately 4 to 5 metres away from the fire.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 text-sm" style="color: #374151;"><span class="font-bold shrink-0" style="color: #C49A28;">3.</span><span>Remove the safety pin from the operating handle and take hold of the applicator.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 text-sm" style="color: #374151;"><span class="font-bold shrink-0" style="color: #C49A28;">4.</span><span>Hold the handle and use a sweeping rotary action to completely envelope the fire with the extinguishing agent.</span></li>
</ul>

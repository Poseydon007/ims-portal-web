<h2 id="s1" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">1. Policy Statement</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #081C2E;">True East Mining Company (TEMC) is committed to the highest standards of Health, Safety, and Environmental performance in every aspect of its mineral exploration, drilling, and related operations in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.</p>

<h2 id="s2" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">2. Our Core Beliefs</h2>

<ul class="list-none pl-0 space-y-1">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Incident Prevention</strong> — all incidents are preventable.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Zero Harm</strong> — zero harm to people is achievable.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Environmental Protection</strong> — environmental protection is non-negotiable.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Quality Excellence</strong> — quality excellence is essential for long-term success.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s3" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">3. Our Strategic Commitments</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">To deliver these beliefs TEMC will:</p>

<ul class="list-none pl-0 space-y-1">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Safe Working Conditions</strong> — provide safe and healthy working conditions to prevent work-related injury and ill health — including fatigue, heat stress, and musculoskeletal disorders — appropriate to the organisation's size and context.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Hazard Elimination</strong> — eliminate hazards and reduce HSE risks using the Hierarchy of Controls: Elimination, Substitution, Engineering, Administrative, and PPE as a last resort.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Legal Compliance</strong> — comply with all applicable legal requirements, including MHRSD regulations, NCEC / MEWA environmental standards, Saudi Mining Investment Law, and client-specific obligations such as Ma'aden Contractor HSE Standards.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Sustainability</strong> — prevent pollution, minimise waste, and promote sustainable resource use across all drilling, exploration, maintenance, and transport activities.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Worker Consultation</strong> — involve workers and representatives in the development, planning, and continual improvement of the IMS.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Objectives and Targets</strong> — establish and regularly review measurable HSE objectives and targets across all relevant functions.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Resource Provision</strong> — provide necessary training, equipment, competent personnel, and infrastructure to achieve our goals.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Safety Culture</strong> — empower all personnel and contractors with Stop Work Authority, allowing them to halt unsafe work without fear of reprisal.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Open Communication</strong> — communicate performance and policy openly with employees, contractors, clients, and regulators.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Continual Improvement</strong> — enhance the IMS through regular audits, management reviews, and incident investigations.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s4" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">4. Application</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #081C2E;">This policy applies to all True East Mining operations, personnel, contractors, and visitors. It is reviewed annually or following significant operational, regulatory, or strategic changes.</p>

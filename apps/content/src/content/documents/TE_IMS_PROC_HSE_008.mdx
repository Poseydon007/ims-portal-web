<h2 id="s1" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">1. Purpose</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">To establish a standardised, safe, and environmentally responsible process for identifying, segregating, storing, handling, transporting, and disposing of all waste — hazardous and non-hazardous — generated at True East Mining Company (TEMC) sites. This procedure ensures strict compliance with ISO 14001:2015 (Clauses 6.1.2 Environmental Aspects, 8.1 Operational Planning and Control), ISO 45001:2018 (Clause 8.1.2), NCEC regulations, and the Saudi Waste Management Law, while minimising environmental impact and actively promoting the waste hierarchy (Reduce, Reuse, Recycle, Recover, Dispose).</p>

<h2 id="s2" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">2. Scope</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This procedure applies to all waste generated by TEMC operations — diamond drilling, exploration, workshops, offices, core yards, and remote desert camps — and by contractors and subcontractors working on behalf of the Company. It covers hazardous waste (used oil, batteries, chemicals, contaminated materials) and non-hazardous waste (general trash, scrap metal, clean drill cuttings, food waste).</p>

<h2 id="s5" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">5. General Safety Rules and Waste Hierarchy</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">Waste is any substance discarded or no longer useful that cannot be put to direct beneficial use. All operations apply the following hierarchy:</p>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Reduce — minimise generation at the source through careful planning, efficient use of materials, and bulk purchasing.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Reuse — reuse materials where safe and practical (e.g. drums for non-hazardous storage, timber for shuttering).</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Recycle — segregate metals, paper, plastics, and oils for recycling via approved contractors.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Recover — recover energy from waste where applicable.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Dispose — landfill or destruction is the last resort, via an NCEC-licensed facility.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Segregation: never mix hazardous and non-hazardous waste under any circumstances.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Spill response: report leaks and spills immediately. Contain and clean using designated spill kits.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>PPE: all personnel wear appropriate PPE when handling waste — nitrile gloves, safety goggles, coveralls, and safety boots.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s6" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">6. Waste Classification</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">Waste is classified as hazardous if it exhibits any of the following characteristics (per NCEC criteria):</p>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Ignitability — flash point &lt;60°C or spontaneously combustible.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Corrosivity — pH ≤2 or ≥12.5, or corrodes steel.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Reactivity — unstable, explosive, or generates toxic gases when mixed with water or heated.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Toxicity — harmful or fatal if absorbed, ingested, or inhaled.</span></li>
</ul>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">TEMC mining examples: used oil and lubricants, batteries, chemical residues, contaminated rags, oily water, and drill cuttings heavily contaminated with hydrocarbons.</p>

<h2 id="s7" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">7. Hazardous Waste Storage</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Containers — use appropriate, labelled containers — UN-approved drums for liquids.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Fill Limits — fill liquid containers to a maximum of 95% capacity to allow headspace for thermal expansion.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Segregation — physically segregate chemically incompatible wastes (acids must be stored separately from bases; oxidisers separate from organics).</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Bunding — store all hazardous liquids in a designated, secure, bunded area with secondary containment capable of holding 110% of the largest container's volume.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Environment — the storage area is clean, well-ventilated, located away from ignition sources, and equipped with spill kits and signage "DANGER – Hazardous Waste – No Unauthorised Access".</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Batteries — store used lead-acid batteries upright in a dry, covered area protected from direct desert sunlight.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Inspections — the HSE team inspects hazardous waste storage areas weekly and records findings.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>90-Day Limit — hazardous waste shall not be stored on site longer than 90 days before being removed by an authorised contractor, unless a longer period is specifically authorised by NCEC.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s8" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">8. Handling and Disposal of Hazardous Waste</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Collection: waste collected at the source in labelled containers.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Internal transport: waste transported to the central storage area by trained personnel only.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>External transport: only an NCEC-licensed waste contractor is engaged for off-site transport and disposal.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Documentation: a formal Waste Manifest is completed for every shipment, signed by the generator, transporter, and final disposal facility.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Retention: all Waste Manifests retained in the IMS for a minimum of 5 years.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Regulatory reporting: the HSE Manager reports to NCEC when unexpectedly large volumes are generated or a reportable environmental spill occurs.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s9" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">9. Non-Hazardous Waste Management</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">Mining examples: office paper, food waste, scrap wood, metal scrap, clean packaging, exhausted tyres, empty non-hazardous containers, general garbage, and clean (non-contaminated) drill cuttings.</p>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Source Segregation — clearly labelled, colour-coded bins at the source (Paper Only, General Waste, Scrap Metal, Plastics).</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Storage — temporarily store non-hazardous waste in a designated, fenced area to prevent wind-blown litter.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Collection — arrange regular collection by municipal authorities or an authorised general waste contractor.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Prohibitions — no open burning or unauthorised dumping of waste is allowed on any TEMC site. Breach is a disciplinary matter.</span></li>
</ul>

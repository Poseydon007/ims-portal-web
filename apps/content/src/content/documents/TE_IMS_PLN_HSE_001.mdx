<div id="s1" class="mt-8 mb-3">
  <h2 class="text-base font-bold mb-1" style="color: #081C2E;">1. Purpose</h2>
  <div style="height: 2px; background-color: #C49A28; width: 100%;"></div>
</div>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-4" style="color: #374151;">This Plan establishes the arrangements by which True East Mining Company (TEMC) prepares for, responds to, and recovers from a Major Emergency at any drilling, exploration, or support site in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. The objective is to protect life first, then the environment, then assets and reputation, and to meet the requirements of ISO 45001:2018, ISO 14001:2015, MHRSD, Saudi Civil Defense, MEWA, NCEC, and TEMC internal standards.</p>

<div id="s2" class="mt-8 mb-3">
  <h2 class="text-base font-bold mb-1" style="color: #081C2E;">2. Scope</h2>
  <div style="height: 2px; background-color: #C49A28; width: 100%;"></div>
</div>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-4" style="color: #374151;">This Plan applies to all TEMC owned and contracted sites, including drilling rigs, core yards, workshops, fuel and chemical storage, camps, vehicles, and access routes. It covers employees, contractors, visitors, and any third parties present on site during an incident. It is read in conjunction with PROC-HSE-004 Site Emergency Preparedness Procedure and site-specific emergency response cards.</p>

<div id="s3" class="mt-8 mb-3">
  <h2 class="text-base font-bold mb-1" style="color: #081C2E;">3. Definitions</h2>
  <div style="height: 2px; background-color: #C49A28; width: 100%;"></div>
</div>

<ul class="space-y-2 mb-4">
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span><strong>Emergency</strong> — an unplanned event that requires immediate response to protect life, environment, or assets.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span><strong>Local Emergency</strong> — an incident that can be managed by the site team using on-site resources (e.g. small fire extinguished with portable extinguisher, first-aid case).</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span><strong>Major Emergency</strong> — an incident that exceeds on-site response capability or threatens life, environment, or assets on a significant scale — requires activation of external emergency services and corporate escalation.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span><strong>On-Site Chief Emergency Controller (OSCEC)</strong> — senior TEMC person on site (SSE / Drilling Superintendent) who assumes overall command of response.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span><strong>Emergency Response Team (ERT)</strong> — trained site personnel assigned to fire, first aid, rescue, spill, and evacuation duties.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span><strong>Assembly Point</strong> — pre-designated safe location upwind of the hazard where personnel muster for headcount.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span><strong>Evacuation</strong> — planned movement of personnel away from a hazard to an assembly point or beyond.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span><strong>All Clear</strong> — the official signal that the emergency is controlled and normal operations may resume.</span></li>
</ul>

  </table>

<div id="s5" class="mt-8 mb-3">
  <h2 class="text-base font-bold mb-1" style="color: #081C2E;">5. Credible Major Emergency Scenarios</h2>
  <div style="height: 2px; background-color: #C49A28; width: 100%;"></div>
</div>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #374151;">The following credible scenarios drive the plan, equipment, training, and drills.</p>

<ul class="space-y-2 mb-4">
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Rig fire (diesel, hydraulics, electrical, wellhead).</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Blowout or loss of well control.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Hydrogen sulphide (H₂S) release — where geology indicates risk.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Dropped load or stuck drill string causing injury or structural failure.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Fuel or chemical spill affecting soil or groundwater.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Medical emergency requiring MedEvac (cardiac, trauma, heat stress).</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Sandstorm or severe dust storm (Shamal) impacting visibility and breathing.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Flash flood in wadi and remote site access routes.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Earthquake — where applicable to the region.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Vehicle rollover on desert or mountain track.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Camp fire, kitchen fire, or food-borne illness outbreak.</span></li>
</ul>

<div id="s6" class="mt-8 mb-3">
  <h2 class="text-base font-bold mb-1" style="color: #081C2E;">6. Precautionary Measures</h2>
  <div style="height: 2px; background-color: #C49A28; width: 100%;"></div>
</div>

<ul class="space-y-2 mb-4">
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Rigs and workshops equipped with fire extinguishers, fire hose reels, sand buckets, and emergency shut-off stations per site fire plan.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Fuel and chemical storage bunded, segregated, ventilated, and fitted with spill kits and grounding per PROC-HSE-007.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>H₂S monitors, SCBA, and escape sets available where formation risk is identified by geology.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>First aid kits, AEDs, stretchers, burn kits, eye-wash stations, and trauma kits at rig floor, camp, and workshop.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Assembly points signed, lit at night, and accessible during sandstorm conditions — located upwind and at least 50 m from identified hazard.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Permit-to-work, JSA, and pre-start checks performed daily to prevent escalation.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Emergency communications — VHF/UHF radios, satellite phone where mobile coverage is absent, landline where available.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Vehicles carry fire extinguisher, first aid kit, water, and emergency contact list.</span></li>
</ul>

    </tbody>

<div id="s9" class="mt-8 mb-3">
  <h2 class="text-base font-bold mb-1" style="color: #081C2E;">9. Scenario Response Cards</h2>
  <div style="height: 2px; background-color: #C49A28; width: 100%;"></div>
</div>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #374151;">Each scenario is supported by a site response card. Summaries below; full cards are held at HSE office, radio room, and rig floor.</p>

<h3 class="text-sm font-bold mt-4 mb-2" style="color: #081C2E;">9.1 Rig Fire</h3>
<ul class="space-y-1.5 mb-4">
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Witness: Raise alarm, shout FIRE FIRE FIRE, call radio.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Driller/Operator: Shut down rig, isolate fuel and power, evacuate crew from rig floor.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>ERT: Attack small fire with extinguisher only if safe; otherwise withdraw and cool surroundings.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>OSCEC: Declare Level 1 or 2; call Civil Defense 998 if Level 2; account for all personnel.</span></li>
</ul>

<h3 class="text-sm font-bold mt-4 mb-2" style="color: #081C2E;">9.2 Blowout / Loss of Well Control</h3>
<ul class="space-y-1.5 mb-4">
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Driller: Initiate well shut-in per PROC-HSE-009 or well-control procedure; alert OSCEC.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>OSCEC: Declare Level 2 or 3; call Civil Defense and client; well-control specialist contractor.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Evacuate non-essential personnel 500 m upwind; establish exclusion zone.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>No ignition sources within exclusion zone — no vehicles, radios, or mobile phones.</span></li>
</ul>

<h3 class="text-sm font-bold mt-4 mb-2" style="color: #081C2E;">9.3 H₂S Release</h3>
<ul class="space-y-1.5 mb-4">
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Alarm at 10 ppm — don SCBA; alarm at 20 ppm — evacuate all non-ERT personnel.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Evacuate crosswind or upwind to Assembly Point.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>ERT with SCBA only may approach to isolate source or rescue casualties.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>OSCEC notifies Civil Defense and client; monitors area until gas reading &lt; 5 ppm.</span></li>
</ul>

<h3 class="text-sm font-bold mt-4 mb-2" style="color: #081C2E;">9.4 Medical / MedEvac</h3>
<ul class="space-y-1.5 mb-4">
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>First aider: Scene safe? Assess ABC. Call for help via radio.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>OSCEC: Call Saudi Red Crescent Ambulance 997; prepare patient for MedEvac (ground or air).</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Clear helicopter pad of loose items if air MedEvac; mark wind direction.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Accompany casualty with medical records and incident summary.</span></li>
</ul>

<h3 class="text-sm font-bold mt-4 mb-2" style="color: #081C2E;">9.5 Fuel / Chemical Spill</h3>
<ul class="space-y-1.5 mb-4">
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Stop the source if safe — close valve, right the drum, plug the leak.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Contain — deploy booms, absorbents, sand, or earth berm. Prevent spread to drains or wadi.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Report — OSCEC notifies HSE Manager; NCEC/MEWA notification for reportable events.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Clean up — place waste in labelled containers; dispose per PROC-HSE-015.</span></li>
</ul>

<h3 class="text-sm font-bold mt-4 mb-2" style="color: #081C2E;">9.6 Sandstorm / Shamal</h3>
<ul class="space-y-1.5 mb-4">
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>On warning: secure tarps, loose items, open ends of pipe racks, ventilation inlets.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>At onset: stop work, shelter in hard-walled buildings or vehicles, close doors and windows.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Wear dust masks (P2 minimum); avoid driving until visibility &gt; 100 m.</span></li>
</ul>

<h3 class="text-sm font-bold mt-4 mb-2" style="color: #081C2E;">9.7 Flash Flood</h3>
<ul class="space-y-1.5 mb-4">
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Evacuate low ground, wadis, and drainage paths immediately.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Move vehicles and equipment to higher ground.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>{"Do NOT attempt to drive through flowing water — turn around, don't drown."}</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Account for personnel; notify OSCEC of any isolated team.</span></li>
</ul>

<h3 class="text-sm font-bold mt-4 mb-2" style="color: #081C2E;">9.8 Earthquake</h3>
<ul class="space-y-1.5 mb-4">
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Drop, cover, hold — away from glass, shelving, pipe racks.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>After shaking stops: evacuate to Assembly Point via safe route; beware of aftershocks.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Do not re-enter damaged structures until inspected by competent person.</span></li>
</ul>

<h3 class="text-sm font-bold mt-4 mb-2" style="color: #081C2E;">9.9 Vehicle Rollover / Road Incident</h3>
<ul class="space-y-1.5 mb-4">
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Turn off ignition, apply handbrake, extract casualties only if vehicle is unstable or on fire.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Radio incident location, number of casualties, nature of injuries.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>Deploy warning triangles; manage traffic if on public road.</span></li>
  <li class="flex items-start gap-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span class="mt-1.5 flex-shrink-0 w-1.5 h-1.5 rounded-full" style="background-color: #C49A28;"></span><span>First aid until ambulance arrives (997).</span></li>
</ul>

<h3 class="text-sm font-bold mt-4 mb-2" style="color: #081C2E;">9.10 Security Incident</h3>

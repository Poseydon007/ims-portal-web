<h2 id="s1" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">1. Purpose</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This procedure defines how True East Mining Company (TEMC) produces, reviews, approves, submits, and archives the HSE Monthly Site Report for each active operation. The report is the primary HSE performance record shared with management, clients, and regulators, and is the evidence base for continual improvement, management review, and compliance with ISO 45001:2018, ISO 14001:2015, and KSA regulatory expectations (MHRSD, MEWA, NCEC).</p>

<h2 id="s2" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">2. Scope</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This procedure applies to all TEMC owned and contracted drilling, exploration, and support operations in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. It covers reports prepared by Site HSE Managers and consolidated at corporate level by the HSE Manager. All employees and contractors providing data to the report are in scope.</p>

<h2 id="s3" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">3. Definitions</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Monthly HSE Report — the formal monthly report covering HSE performance, incidents, inspections, training, environment, and man-hours for a TEMC site.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>LTI (Lost Time Injury) — work-related injury resulting in absence from work on the next full shift or later.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>MTC (Medical Treatment Case) — injury requiring medical treatment beyond first aid, without lost time.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>FAC (First Aid Case) — injury treated by first aid only.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>TRIFR — Total Recordable Injury Frequency Rate per 1,000,000 hours worked (Fatalities + LTI + RWC + MTC).</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>LTIFR — Lost Time Injury Frequency Rate per 1,000,000 hours worked.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Near Miss — an event that could have resulted in harm but did not.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>HiPo (High-Potential Event) — an incident or near miss with the potential to cause serious or fatal harm.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Man-Hours Worked — total hours worked by employees and contractors on site during the reporting period.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s7" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">7. Performance Metric Formulae</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>LTIFR — = (Number of LTIs ÷ Total hours worked) × 1,000,000</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>TRIFR — = ((Fatalities + LTI + RWC + MTC) ÷ Total hours worked) × 1,000,000</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Severity Rate — = (Days lost ÷ Total hours worked) × 1,000,000</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Near-Miss Ratio — = Near misses ÷ Recordable injuries. A healthy reporting culture has a ratio ≥ 10:1.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>All incidents reported to site are included — omission of any incident is treated as a reporting breach.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s8" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">8. Data Collection, Validation, and Quality Control</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>The Site HSE Officer cross-checks all data for internal consistency — hours, headcount, incident counts, training register.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Incidents are reconciled with the Flash Reports issued during the month. Every Flash must appear in the monthly.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Environmental data is reconciled with waste manifests and fuel records.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Corrective action dates are validated against the CAR register.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Any discrepancy identified after submission is corrected with a formal erratum to the same distribution list.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s9" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">9. Review and Approval Workflow</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Step 1 — Compile draft — Site HSE Officer completes draft using TE-IMS-FRM-HSE-033 Monthly HSE Report template.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Step 2 — Internal HSE review — Site HSE Manager reviews for accuracy, completeness, and tone.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Step 3 — SSE review — Drilling Superintendent or SSE reviews operational entries, signs off.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Step 4 — Corporate consolidation — Corporate HSE Manager consolidates multiple sites, adds company-level commentary.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Step 5 — MD approval — Managing Director receives and signs off before external distribution.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Step 6 — Distribution — To client / regulator (where applicable), saved to IMS master.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Step 7 — Site communication — Key findings shared in toolbox talks and HSE noticeboards.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s10" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">10. Submission and Filing</h2>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Submit final report by the 5th working day of the following month to the required client portal and/or regulator.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Controlled copy filed in the IMS master by date: IMS/Records/HSE-Monthly/YYYY-MM/.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Retention: minimum 5 years (longer if client contract requires).</span></li>
</ul>

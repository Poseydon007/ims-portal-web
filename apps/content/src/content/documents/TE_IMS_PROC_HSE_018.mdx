<h2 id="s1" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">1. Purpose</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">To establish a systematic methodology for identifying environmental aspects of True East Mining's activities, products and services, evaluating their impacts, determining significance and keeping the information current. This procedure implements ISO 14001:2015 Clause 6.1.2 (Environmental Aspects) and underpins the company's environmental objectives, operational controls and legal compliance in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.</p>

<h2 id="s2" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">2. Scope</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This procedure applies to all True East Mining activities, products and services across exploration drilling, core handling, camp operations, vehicle fleet, laydown yards, supply chain and decommissioning. It covers environmental aspects under normal, abnormal (start-up/shut-down) and emergency conditions, and both direct aspects under TE control and indirect aspects influenced through contractors, suppliers and interested parties.</p>

<h2 id="s5" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">5. Aspect Identification Process</h2>

<h3 class="text-sm font-bold mt-5 mb-2" style="color: #081C2E;">5.1 Approach</h3>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">The HSE Manager (EMR), together with Department Heads, conducts a comprehensive review of all activities, products and services at least annually and whenever a trigger under Section 5.3 occurs. ISO 14001 awareness training is delivered before each review cycle.</p>

<h3 class="text-sm font-bold mt-5 mb-2" style="color: #081C2E;">5.2 Identification Methods</h3>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Interviews and walkthroughs with employees and supervisors.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Site inspections and environmental walk-downs.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Review of documents, procedures and operational records.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Monitoring and measurement data — emissions, waste, water, fuel use.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Review of legal register and regulatory notices.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Review of previous environmental incidents, complaints and corrective actions.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Interested-party feedback and community communications.</span></li>
</ul>

<h3 class="text-sm font-bold mt-5 mb-2" style="color: #081C2E;">5.3 Follow-up Reviews (Triggers)</h3>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">The aspect register is re-evaluated whenever one or more of the following conditions occur:</p>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Change in process, activity, product, service or methodology.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Significant change in the scope or mission of the organisation.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Expansion of existing activities — new rig, new site, new campaign.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>New or changed legal / regulatory requirements (MHRSD, NCEC, MEWA).</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Occurrence of an environmental incident, near miss or complaint.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Management Review outcomes or Management decision.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul class="space-y-1 mb-4 list-none pl-0">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>At minimum, annually.</span></li>
</ul>

<h3 class="text-sm font-bold mt-5 mb-2" style="color: #081C2E;">7.2 Quantitative SSPD Assessment</h3>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">Aspects that do not trigger the default categories are rated using the SSPD method: Scale, Severity, Probability and Duration — each scored 1 to 4. The Risk Priority Number (RPN) is the sum of the four scores. Aspects with RPN ≥10 are considered significant.</p>

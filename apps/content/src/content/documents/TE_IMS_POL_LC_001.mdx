<h2 id="s1" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">1. Policy Statement</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;">True East Mining Company (TEMC) is fully committed to supporting the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 and the National Transformation Program by maximising local content and prioritising Saudi nationals in employment, training, and business opportunities. TEMC views Saudization (Nitaqat) and local content development as a strategic priority that strengthens operations and competitiveness in the Saudi market.</p>

<h2 id="s2" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">2. Objectives</h2>

<ul class="list-none pl-0 space-y-1">
  <li class="flex gap-2 text-sm" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28;">•</span><span>Achieve high Saudization levels across all job categories in compliance with MHRSD Nitaqat and client-specific targets (e.g. Ma'aden, the Saudi Mining Services Company).</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 text-sm" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28;">•</span><span>Increase the participation of Saudi nationals in skilled, technical, and managerial roles — geology, drilling, HSE, maintenance, and management.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 text-sm" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28;">•</span><span>Develop Saudi talent through structured training, career progression, and mentorship.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 text-sm" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28;">•</span><span>Maximise local procurement of goods and services where quality, safety, and cost are competitive.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 text-sm" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28;">•</span><span>Contribute to the Ministry of Industry and Mineral Resources (MIM) local content reporting and the Local Content and Government Procurement Authority (LCGPA) framework.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s3" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">3. Key Principles and Commitments</h2>

<ul class="list-none pl-0 space-y-1">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Saudization Targets</strong> — prioritise Saudi nationals in recruitment and set annual percentage targets by job category. Progress is tracked monthly and reported at Management Review.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Training and Development</strong> — provide mandatory programmes to build capabilities in drilling, maintenance, HSE, and management, including on-the-job training (OJT), cross-training, and structured succession planning.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Local Procurement</strong> — give preference to Saudi-owned or Saudi-majority suppliers and contractors where quality, safety, delivery, and cost are competitive. Track and report local content percentages in all procurement activities.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Non-Discrimination</strong> — all recruitment and promotion decisions are based on merit and competency. Discrimination on the basis of race, gender, nationality, religion, or any protected characteristic is strictly prohibited.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Career Progression</strong> — Saudi nationals are given preference for internal promotion, sponsored certifications, and high-potential development schemes.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s4" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">4. Monitoring and Reporting</h2>

<ul class="list-none pl-0 space-y-1">
  <li class="flex gap-2 text-sm" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28;">•</span><span>Maintain a Saudization and Local Content Register.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 text-sm" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28;">•</span><span>Monthly reporting to senior management and quarterly Management Review against Nitaqat targets.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 text-sm" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28;">•</span><span>Timely submission of required reports to MHRSD, MIM, and clients.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 text-sm" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28;">•</span><span>Annual external audit of Saudization compliance status.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s5" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">5. Training and Communication</h2>

<ul class="list-none pl-0 space-y-1">
  <li class="flex gap-2 text-sm" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28;">•</span><span>All employees receive awareness training on this policy at induction.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 text-sm" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28;">•</span><span>The policy is displayed in Arabic and English at all TEMC sites and communicated through toolbox talks (PROC-HSE-007).</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 text-sm" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28;">•</span><span>Progress is communicated openly with the workforce and celebrated publicly when targets are met.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s1" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">1. PURPOSE</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This procedure establishes a systematic process for:</p>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Identifying opportunities for continual improvement of the Integrated Management System (IMS).</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Addressing non-conformities, incidents, near misses, and other undesirable situations.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Implementing corrective actions to eliminate root causes.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Preventing recurrence through preventive actions and systemic improvements.</span></li>
</ul>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">It ensures compliance with:</p>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>ISO 9001:2015 (clause 10.3) — Continual improvement.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>ISO 14001:2015 (clause 10.3) — Continual improvement.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>ISO 45001:2018 (clause 10.2) — Incident, non-conformity and corrective action.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s2" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">2. SCOPE</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">This procedure applies to:</p>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>All non-conformities (major and minor), incidents, accidents, and near misses.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Opportunities for improvement identified from audits, management reviews, employee suggestions, incident investigations, performance data, or external feedback.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Corrective and preventive actions arising from any source (internal audits, client complaints, regulatory findings, inspections).</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s3" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">3. DEFINITIONS</h2>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Non-conformity — Non-fulfilment of a requirement (e.g., missing PPE, expired training, procedural deviation).</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Corrective Action — Action taken to eliminate the cause of a detected non-conformity and prevent recurrence.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Preventive Action — Proactive action taken to eliminate the cause of a potential non-conformity before it occurs.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>CAPA — Corrective Action / Preventive Action.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Root Cause — The underlying reason for a non-conformity, identified using structured techniques (5 Whys, fishbone, fault tree).</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s4" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">4. RESPONSIBILITIES</h2>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>IMS Project Lead / Management Representative — Coordinate the CAPA process, maintain the CAPA Register, and verify effectiveness of closed actions.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>HSE Manager — Lead investigation of safety and environmental non-conformities; assign and track corrective actions.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Department Heads / Process Owners — Identify root causes, implement corrective actions in their area, and verify effectiveness.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>All Personnel — Report non-conformities, incidents, near misses, and improvement opportunities immediately.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>CEO / Managing Director — Review high-risk CAPAs in Management Review Meetings and allocate necessary resources.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s5" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">5. PROCEDURE STEPS</h2>

<h2 class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">1. Identify &amp; Report — Any employee or contractor identifies a non-conformity, incident, near miss, or improvement opportunity. Reports are submitted immediately using:</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">› TE-IMS-FRM-HSE-002 — Incident / Accident Flash Notification (for incidents).</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">› TE-IMS-FRM-HSE-003 — Near Miss Report Form (for near misses).</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">› Verbal or email notice to supervisor / HSE for minor non-conformities and improvement ideas.</p>

<h2 class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">2. Evaluate &amp; Classify — IMS Project Lead / HSE Manager evaluates within 24 hours:</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">› Immediate containment actions (stop work, secure area, first aid).</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">› Severity classification — Major (risk of fatality, major spill, regulatory violation) or Minor (low risk).</p>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">› Assigns CAPA number in the CAPA Register (TE-IMS-REG-SYS-002).</p>

<h2 class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">3. Investigate Root Cause — Use a structured method (5 Whys, fishbone diagram, fault tree analysis). Involve relevant personnel (witnesses, operators, supervisors).</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">Risk-based investigation teams:</p>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Minor Non-conformities: Investigation managed by the relevant Department Head.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Major Non-conformities: Require a formal multi-disciplinary team (HSE, Operations, Maintenance) for comprehensive failure analysis.</span></li>
</ul>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed mb-3" style="color: #081C2E;">The Systemic Root Cause / “6th Why” rule:</p>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Investigations must push beyond “human error” as a final cause.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Auditors and investigators must identify systemic failures (e.g., inadequate budget, poor policy, cultural gaps, missing training).</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Root cause(s) are documented in the CAPA Report (TE-IMS-FRM-SYS-003).</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">4. Develop &amp; Approve Actions — Determine corrective actions (eliminate root cause) and preventive actions (avoid similar issues). Assign owner, deadline, and verification method. Approve actions: HSE Manager for minor; CEO for major.</h2>

<h2 class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">5. Implement Actions — Owner executes actions within the agreed deadline. Update affected documents (procedures, SOPs, forms) if required.</h2>

<h2 id="s6" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">6. Verify Effectiveness — IMS Project Lead verifies actions are implemented and effective (e.g., no recurrence within 3 months). Document verification in the CAPA Report and close the CAPA in the Register.</h2>

<h2 id="s7" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">7. Communicate &amp; Share Lessons — Communicate lessons learned via Toolbox Talks (TE-IMS-PROC-HSE-007). Include major findings in the Management Review (TE-IMS-PROC-SYS-002).</h2>

<h2 class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">6. RECORDS &amp; RETENTION</h2>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>CAPA Register, reports, and verification evidence are retained for a minimum of 5 years.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Stored in a secure, controlled location within the IMS repository.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Tracked in the Master Document Register (TE-IMS-REG-SYS-001).</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">7. MONITORING &amp; CONTINUAL IMPROVEMENT</h2>

<ul class="list-none mb-3 space-y-1">
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Track KPIs: percentage of CAPAs closed on time, recurrence rate, number of improvement opportunities implemented.</span></li>
  <li class="text-sm leading-relaxed flex gap-2" style="color: #081C2E;"><span style="color: #C49A28; flex-shrink: 0;">▸</span><span>Review effectiveness of this procedure annually during the Management Review Meeting.</span></li>
</ul>

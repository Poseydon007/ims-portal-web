<h2 id="s1" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">1. Policy Statement</h2>

<p class="text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;">True East Mining Company (TEMC) conducts business with the highest standards of integrity, honesty, and ethical behaviour. TEMC's reputation, eligibility for tenders, and client relationships depend on lawful and ethical conduct at all times. TEMC has zero tolerance for bribery, corruption, fraud, or unethical behaviour.</p>

<h2 id="s2" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">2. Our Commitment</h2>

<ul class="list-none pl-0 space-y-1">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Comply with Law</strong> — adhere to the Saudi Anti-Bribery Law (Royal Decree M/43), the Saudi Penal Code, the Combating Bribery Law, and international standards including the US FCPA and UK Bribery Act where applicable.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Prohibit Bribery</strong> — forbid the offering, promising, giving, accepting, or soliciting of any bribe, kickback, or facilitation payment to influence a business decision.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Transparency</strong> — maintain accurate and transparent books and records; no false entries to conceal improper payments.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Integrity Culture</strong> — promote a "Speak Up" culture where concerns can be raised without fear of retaliation.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s3" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">3. Key Prohibitions</h2>

<ul class="list-none pl-0 space-y-1">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Gifts and Hospitality</strong> — no gifts or entertainment may be accepted if they influence business decisions. Items above SAR 500 require pre-approval in writing and documentation on the gift register.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Political and Charitable Contributions</strong> — no political or charitable contributions may be made to secure business advantages; charitable donations require MD approval.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Third Parties</strong> — prohibit engagement of agents, consultants, or intermediaries to make improper payments on TEMC's behalf. Third-party due diligence applies to all material relationships.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span><strong>Conflicts of Interest</strong> — all employees disclose actual or potential conflicts of interest to the MD; undisclosed conflicts are a disciplinary matter.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s4" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">4. Reporting and Non-Retaliation</h2>

<ul class="list-none pl-0 space-y-1">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Suspected violations must be reported to a supervisor, the HR Manager, the Management Representative, or the confidential Speak Up hotline.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Good-faith reporters are protected — no retaliation of any kind, including adverse performance decisions.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Confirmed violations result in disciplinary action up to and including termination and, where warranted, referral to the competent authorities.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s5" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">5. Training and Records</h2>

<ul class="list-none pl-0 space-y-1">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>All personnel receive ethics and anti-bribery training at induction and annually, tracked via the Training and Competence Matrix (REG-TRN-001).</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Gifts and hospitality above SAR 500, conflicts of interest, and approved charitable donations are logged on the Ethics Register.</span></li>
</ul>

<h2 id="s6" class="text-sm font-bold mt-8 mb-3 pb-2" style="color: #081C2E; border-bottom: 2px solid #C49A28;">6. Governance and Review</h2>

<ul class="list-none pl-0 space-y-1">
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>This policy is owned by the Managing Director and reviewed annually or on any material regulatory or corporate change.</span></li>
  <li class="flex gap-2 mb-2 text-sm leading-relaxed" style="color: #374151;"><span style="color: #C49A28; margin-top: 2px;">•</span><span>Effectiveness is reported at the Management Review (PROC-SYS-002).</span></li>
</ul>
